About Us

Rogue by Design Apparel also known as RDA, is a small buisness with big dreams.
The dream being designs from our favorite franchises, in the form of good looking and comfortable clothing.
A way for anyone anywhere regardless of gender to be able to sport their passion!
Each shirt has fitted, and non-fitted options. So no matter what your style is, we have it.
All designs are pressed on to 100% recycled cotton tees, using a machine called a Cricut.
Our clothing is meant to last, and can hold up in any wash so dont be afraid to get it dirty.
We hope you enjoy our products, and express your inner geek with us!
